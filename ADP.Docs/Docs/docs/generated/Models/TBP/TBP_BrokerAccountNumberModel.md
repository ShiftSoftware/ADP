Represents a broker's account number at a specific company.
 Brokers may have different account numbers for different dealer companies they work with.

| Property | Summary |
|----------|---------|
| AccountNumber <div><strong>``string``</strong></div> | The account number assigned to the broker at the company. |
| CompanyID <div><strong>``long?``</strong></div> | The company ID this account number belongs to. |
